# Product Overview

## Product Name
Hello There

## What It Is
A minimal, single-section landing page with a clean "Hello there" greeting. Simple, direct, and fast — no clutter, no navigation, no extra sections.

## Users / Audience
Anyone who needs a clean, instant web presence — a personal page, a placeholder, or a simple welcome screen.

## Positioning
The simplest landing page possible: one section, one message, zero noise.

## Brand & Tone
- Calm, minimal, welcoming
- Clean and airy — whitespace is intentional
- Friendly but not loud

## Scope (MVP)
- 1 section
- Headline text: "Hello there"
- Simple, readable layout

## Strategic Principles
- Less is more: no navigation, no footer bloat, no distracting elements
- Fast to load, easy to read
- The single "Hello there" text is the product

## Core Workflow
A visitor lands on the page and immediately sees the "Hello there" greeting. That's the complete interaction — no further steps required.
